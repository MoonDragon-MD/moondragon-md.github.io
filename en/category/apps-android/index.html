Televisioni

Posted by MD     Category: Apps Android

App to watch streaming of some Italian televisions; most channels are from the official web pages, so from abroad they won’t work (unless you use a VPN with Italian server)! If you should see badly in the internal viewer, especially on tablets, use the share icon to open them with the desired browser.

 

A variety of national and regional channels are included, plus I have put links to alternative sites where you can watch other channels.
